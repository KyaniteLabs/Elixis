AI identity, brand voice, design system, and naming generator

Elixis is a local-first pattern synthesis engine from Kyanite Labs. It turns raw references, influences, values, aesthetics, constraints, people, works, and places into a reusable pattern graph.

  • Brand voice, positioning language, and design system direction from the same reference set
  • Naming research, semantic fit checks, and aligned name variants
